Complaints against Navient Solutions, LLC, have decreased since 2017 lawsuit

By Rachel McCrea

According to data from the Consumer Financial Protection Bureau's Consumer Complaint Database , the number of complaints against student loan company Navient Solutions, LLC have dropped since peaking in January 2017, the year a successful lawsuit was filed agianst them. Since then, complaint trends have also shifted.

Navient has a lengthy history of consumer complaints. A years-long lawsuit was settled in early 2022, mandating they pay almost $2 billion to borrowers wronged by the company.

According to an explainer from the website Student Loan Planner , the Consumer Financial Protection Bureau accused Navient of cheating the system and leading student loan borrowers astray.
